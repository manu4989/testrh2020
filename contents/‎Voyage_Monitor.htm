Route Explorer

This map shows you previous routes the ship has travelled. It helps you to understand the history of your ship and is also a way to access ship dashboards (see Dashboards).

To view Route History, you can access Route Explorer via two different paths:

Double click on a ship from the map, then on Route Explorer. The system will automatically select the last month in the calendar selector and will show the route(s) that the vessel did in the last month. User can change the selected period as he may wish

Side Menu > Map > Route Explorer. Select a ship and click ok to apply your selection. Then click on . The map automatically zooms in on the ship.

Voyage Legs

If you select one or more ships, filter a period of time and click on Apply button, you can see a list of voyages (from Previous Port to Next Port) performed by the vessel(s) in that period.

Clicking on the "eye" on the right of each row --> the detail of the single voyage (from/to; start/end; phases port/man/sea...) will appear.
